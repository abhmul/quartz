# Definition
Let $X$ be a [[Set]] and let $\{x_{\alpha}\}_{\alpha \in A} \subset X$ be a [[Net]]. We say $\{y_{\beta}\}_{\beta \in B} \subset X$ is a [[Subnet]] of $\{x_{\alpha}\}_{\alpha \in A}$ if $\exists n: B \to A$ s.t. 
1. $n$ is an [[Order-Preserving Function]].
2. $n$ is a [[Final Function]].
3. $y_{\bullet} = x_{\bullet} \circ n$. That is $y_{\beta}= x_{n(\beta)}$ for all $\beta \in B$.
