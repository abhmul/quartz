# Definition
Let $V$ be an [[Inner Product Space]] over $\mathbb{R}$ and let $P \subset V$ be a [[Convex Polytope]]. A [[Minimal Face]] of $P$ is a [[Convex Polytope Face|face]] of $P$ that does not contain any other [[Proper Face]] of $P$.
