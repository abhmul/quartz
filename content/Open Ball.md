# Definition
Let $(M, d)$ be a [[Nonempty]] [[Metric Space]] and let $x \in M$. Then an [[Open Ball]] about $x$ of radius $\epsilon \in \mathbb{R}_{>0}$ is the [[Set]]:
$$B_{\epsilon}(x) := \{y \in M : d(x,y) < \epsilon\}.$$
