# Definition
Let $(M, d)$ be a [[Metric Space]]. We say $({x}_{n})_{n=1}^{\infty} \subset M$ is a [[Cauchy Sequence]] if $\forall \epsilon> 0$ ther exists $N \in \mathbb{N}$ so that for all $n,m \geq N$, $d(x_{n}, x_{m}) < \epsilon$.
