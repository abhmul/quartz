---
# Definition
Let $S, R$ be a [[Set]]s, and let $\{f_{\alpha}\in R^{S}\}_{\alpha \in A}$ and $\{b_{\alpha} \in R\}_{\alpha \in A}$ for some [[Index Set]] $A$. Then we call 
$$f_{\alpha}(x)= b_{\alpha} \text{ }\forall \alpha \in A$$
an [[Equation System]].
