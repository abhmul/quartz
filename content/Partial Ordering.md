# Definition
Let $P$ be a [[Set]] and let $\leq$ be an [[Order Relation]] on $P$. Then $(P, \leq)$ is a [[Partial Ordering]].

## Remarks
1. We can define the [[Relation]] $<$ by simply stating for $x, y \in P$ that $x < y$ [[If and Only If]] $x \leq y$ and $x \neq y$.
