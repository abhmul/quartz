# Statement
Suppose $X, Y$ are [[Topological Space]]s and $X \cong Y$. Then $X$ is [[Path-Connected]] [[If and Only If]] $Y$ is.

## Proof
[[Continuous Functions Preserve Path-Connectedness]] $\blacksquare$
