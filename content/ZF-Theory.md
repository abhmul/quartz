# Definition
The [[Theory]] made up of the following [[Axiom]]s and [[Axiom Schema]]s:
1. [[Axiom of Extensionality]]
2. [[Axiom of Regularity]]
3. [[Axiom Schema of Specification]]
4. [[Axiom of Pairing]]
5. [[Axiom of Union]]
6. [[Axiom Schema of Replacement]]
7. [[Axiom of Infinity]]
8. [[Axiom of Power Set]]
