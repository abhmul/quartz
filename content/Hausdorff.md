# Definition
Let $(X, \tau)$ be a [[Topological Space]]. Then $X$ is [[Hausdorff]] if for every $x, y \in X$ there exist $U, V \in \tau$ s.t. $x \in U$, $y \in V$, and $U \cap V = \emptyset$

Also known as a 
- *T2 Space*
- *Seperated Space*
