# Definition
Let $(X, \mathcal{M}, \mu)$ be a [[Measure Space]]. Then the [[Set]] of [[L-Plus Functions]] is defined as

$$L^{+} = \{f:  X \to [0, \infty]: f \text{ is }\mathcal{M}\text{-measureable}\}$$
