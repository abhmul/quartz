# Definition

Let $f: \mathbb{R} \to M$ where $(M, d)$ is a [[Metric Space]]. Then $f$ is [[Right-Continuous]] at $x \in \mathbb{R}$ if

$$\lim_{y \downarrow x} f(y) = f(x)$$
$f$ is [[Right-Continuous]] if it is [[Right-Continuous]] at all $x \in \mathbb{R}$.
