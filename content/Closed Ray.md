# Definition
Let $(T, \leq)$ be a [[Total Ordering]].  A [[Closed Ray]] is a [[Set]] $R \subset T$ of the form $R := \{x \in T : x \leq a\}$ or $R := \{x \in T : x \geq a\}$ for some $a \in T$.

## Remarks
1. We denote $[a, \rightarrow) := \{x \in T : x \geq a\}$ and $(\leftarrow,a] := \{x \in T : x \leq a\}$.
