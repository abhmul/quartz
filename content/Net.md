# Definition
Let $X$ be a [[Set]] and [[Index Set]] $A$ a [[Directed Partial Ordering]]. Then [[Function]] $x_{\bullet}: A \to X$ is a [[Net]]. We often denote $x_{\bullet}$ as an indexed subset of $X$ in the form $\{x_{\alpha}\}_{\alpha \in A} \subset X$.
