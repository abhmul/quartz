# Definition
We define $c_0$ as
$$c_{0} := \{({x}_{n})_{n=1}^{\infty} \subset \mathbb{R} : \lim\limits_{n \in \mathbb{N}} x_{n} \to 0\}$$
equipped with the norm $||x||_{\infty} := \sup\limits_{n \in \mathbb{N}} |x_{n}|$ for $x \in l_{\infty}$.
