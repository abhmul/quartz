# Definition
A [[Lebesgue-Stieltjes Measure]] is a [[Borel Measure]] $\mu$ on $\mathcal{B}(\mathbb{R})$ s.t. $\mu(I) < \infty$ for all [[Bounded Set|bounded]] [[Interval]]s of $\mathbb{R}$.
